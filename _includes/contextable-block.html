Contextable is an app for creating and reviewing flashcards. I made it to learn Russian, my partner's native language.

  • Swipe left and right on your stack to review your cards with an intutive user interface.
  • Put your cards in context by attaching examples, defintions, or any other kind of text.
  • Record and playback audio for any card or context.
